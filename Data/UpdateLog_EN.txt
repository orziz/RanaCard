V1.3.0
Additions
1. Added the option to disable automatic play for cards in hand. Right-click to toggle disable/enable, as shown. When disabled, the card won't be auto-played via the Quick Play gear.
2. Added the option to disable pendants. Right-click to toggle disable/enable, as shown. When disabled, the pendant's effects are suspended. (Pendants with on-acquire effects or negative impacts cannot be disabled.)
3. Added hotkeys for Quick Discard and Quick Sell. Hover your mouse over a card in hand to use these shortcuts. Defaults to no keybind; customize in Settings > Controls.
4. Added batch purchasing for plot expansions. When using a shovel in the shop to expand plots, you can now buy multiple at once.
5. Added long-press functionality to the difficulty button for continuous switching between difficulties.
6. Added a counter to the pendant [Power of Orange].
7. Added the new pendant [Raindrop Blossom Plantation].
8. Added achievements [Apex Plant], [Apex Animal], and [Apex Orange]. Unlock by clearing the highest difficulty for each class (if already completed, relaunch the game to unlock).
